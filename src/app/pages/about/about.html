About Us

Loyalty Lawncare is a locally owned and operated business committed to delivering top-tier outdoor services with integrity and pride. Whether you're looking for regular maintenance or seasonal help, we treat every yard like it's our own — with loyalty, consistency, and care.

The business was formed at the urging of a proud dad who wanted to see his three sons—Jackson, Will, and Wesley—build something together. With a shared love for being outdoors—whether it’s hunting, playing baseball, creek fishing, or their annual mission trips together—it made sense to turn a passion for the outdoors into a business. It’s honest work, outside in the sun, with visible results and time to do it right. The boys aren’t trying to build a big company or grow into a fleet of trucks. They’re focused on quality, not quantity—earning enough to buy a rusty old truck and maybe a boat along the way.

At the end of the day, Loyalty Lawncare is just three brothers who care about doing a good job, treating customers right, and enjoying the work while they’re at it.

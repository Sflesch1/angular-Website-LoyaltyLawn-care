Our Services

  • Mowing – Weekly or one-time cuts available. We are confident that when you see the shape of your lawn when you get back from your trip that you will want us to come back.
  • Trimming – Edging around walkways, flowerbeds, and fences. Also avaliable for hedge and bush trimming.
  • Gutter Cleaning – Leaf and debris removal for proper drainage. We recommend this service annually for peace of mind as it comes with inspection images.
  • Driveway and Sidewalk Pressure Washing - We remove dirt, oil, and grime and can add this onto our visit to save you time coordinating with other services.
  • Weeding & Mulching – Clean out garden beds, freshen up landscaping, and replace post storm mulch loss.
  • Waste & Leaf Removal – Seasonal cleanup and haul-away. We can also run your small junk pile to the dump for you as an add on service.
